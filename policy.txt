Privacy Policy for Net Salary App

Last updated: November 22, 2023

At Net Salary, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you how we look after your personal data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1. What Data We Collect
Net Salary App is a financial tool designed to calculate net salary. We do not collect or store any personal information.

2. Data We Do Not Collect
We do not collect sensitive personal data such as name, address, social security number, or any other personal identifier. The calculations are performed locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Data
Since we do not collect personal data, there is no use of such data.

4. Data Security
We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. The app functions offline, and no data is stored on our servers.

5. Data Retention
We do not retain any personal data as none is collected.

6. Your Legal Rights
You have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to access, correction, erasure, restriction, transfer, to object to processing, to portability of data, and (where the lawful ground of processing is consent) to withdraw consent.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible.

8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at ruy_fusion@hotmail.com.

This privacy policy does not apply to data we collect through other means (including offline) or from other sources.
